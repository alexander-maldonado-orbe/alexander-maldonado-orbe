## 🧬 About Me

I am a **computational biologist** and **biotechnologist** from Ecuador, passionate about understanding the emergence, spread, and evolution of viral pathogens through the lens of **phylodynamics** and **spatial epidemiology**.

My research bridges **molecular virology** (viral isolation, RT-qPCR, cell culture) with **computational methods** (Bayesian phylogenetics, spatial clustering, reproducible data analysis in R). I have worked extensively on **rabies virus**, **bluetongue virus (BTV)** , and **vesicular stomatitis virus (VSV)** in the context of livestock and public health in Latin America.

🔬 **Current position:** Research Faculty at Universidad Internacional SEK, Quito, Ecuador  
🏥 **Biobank lead:** Establishing an ISO 20387-aligned biobank for infectious disease research  
🎯 **Next goal:** Pursuing a PhD in **Molecular Epidemiology** or **Computational Virology** in Europe
